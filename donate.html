On 26 August 2016, NGO First Human Rights Foundation was founded by Socialist Activist, as a non-governmental, non-profit and non-political organization. The organization was established as the will of citizens to participate actively in Protection of Human Rights , as well as to take an active role in the civil society development through protection of civil Liberties. First Human Rights Foundation Registered with central government of india with registered no. 166151/16 .FHRF is dedicated for protection of human rights and promotion ,upliftment of economical backward people ,education , peace, national integration by communication between people . FHRF are dedicated to spread human rights value in to every part of india. Legal Services:- FHRF provides legal service to weaker section of society. FHRF organize LOK ADALAT for amicable settlement of dispute. FHRF focus on education , health, legal Aid, employment, child labour , women empowerm.

OUR APPROACH

FHRF Will strive to bring together all People who believe in democracy and human rights values in – social , political , economical area. FHRF Shall be non political , non government organization. FHRF Promote Health , Education , legal awareness in to society and work for structural changes to fulfill Eco-social and political aspirations of all people and protection of their ethic, religious and cultural rights. FHRF educate People about their fundamental rights, civil liberties. FHRF strive to the independence and impartiality of the judiciary freedom of the Press and other media and right to have access to information. FHRF focus on national and international problems in their depth and complexity. FHRF strive for promoting a secular, democratic and just society and people’s participation in the decision making process at every level of society where people can raise their voices on different issue.
